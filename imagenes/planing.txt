In Alien Invasion , the player controls a rocket ship that appears at the bottom center of the screen.
The player can move the ship right and left using arrow keys and shoot bullets using the spacebar. When the game begins, a fleet of aliens fills the sky and move across and down the screen. 
The player shoots and destroy the aliens.  f the players shoot all the aliens, new fleet appears that moves faster.
If the aliens hits the ship or it reaches the bottom of the screen it loses a ship. There will be 3 ships.
